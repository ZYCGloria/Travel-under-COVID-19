TRAVEL UNDER COVID-19

DOMESTIC, INTERNATIONAL, MORE...

Concerns about COVID-19 infection, combined with the control measures of population movement, have hit the global travel and transport industry hard in a short time.

With the easing of the epidemic in China, domestic tourism is accelerating its recovery. However, the high death toll and severe situation abroad make international tourism still face great challenges.
